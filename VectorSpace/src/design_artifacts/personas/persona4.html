Persona Four

Scott, Client/Server Infrastructure Specialist


Scott Newsom is a Client/Server Infrastructure Specialist with an Associate’s Degree in Computer Science, which he completed in 1996. In his role, Scott focuses primarily on workflow automation and scripting, often facing the challenge of navigating corporate politics that can sometimes complicate his projects. His approach to work is organized and self-directed; he doesn’t struggle to start or maintain focus during his tasks. To stay productive, he regularly uses tools like OneNote, Microsoft Teams, and Devolutions Remote Server Manager, relying on these applications to structure his day.

Scott has little need for announcements or team-wide communications but appreciates features that allow for organization and efficient task management, such as Vector Space’s scheduling and task lists. Though he doesn’t require custom-developed modules, Scott values the flexibility of customizable dashboards that help him tailor his workspace to meet his specific needs. His ideal platform would further support his focus-driven approach, with options for decluttering his workspace and integrating quick access to frequently used tools. Scott’s motivation centers on a streamlined, distraction-free work environment where he can achieve peak productivity and manage his workflow independently, minimizing the impact of corporate obstacles.
