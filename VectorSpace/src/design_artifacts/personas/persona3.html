Persona Three

Nathaniel, a bioengineering student


Nathaniel, age 22, is a full-time student completing his Bachelor's degree in Bioengineering with a minor in Mathematics. He was born and raised in Pennsylvania, and is studying at a small university in Kentucky. He currently lives in a dorm with a roommate. He spends a lot of time studying in his dorm or the library, usually with friends.

As a 22-year-old and an engineering student, Nathaniel is quite handy with technology. Nathaniel's main tasks involve checking his email for messages in Outlook a few times a day and tracking assignments, exam dates, and such on his Moodle dashboard. He wishes that there was a way he could merge his calendars so he could keep track of school and personal commitments in a single calendar. While Nathaniel doesn't use productivity apps, he is interested in using VectorSpace's collaborative tools such as custom modules for team goals and metrics, prioritized announcements, notification filtering, and productivity visualizations to track peak productivity. Customizable dashboards are a high priority, allowing him to view essential tools and data in one place, and as he frequently checks for updates and sends announcements in group settings, he sees Vector Space as an ideal platform to stay organized, productive, and aligned with his academic goals.
