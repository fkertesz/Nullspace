Persona Five

Drew Stewart, Undergraduate Student


Drew Stewart is a full-time undergraduate student majoring in both Computer Science and Data Science, with a minor in Business, and is set to graduate in May 2025. Drew’s daily responsibilities include personal organization—such as cooking, morning routines, and taking medications—which are essential to starting the day productively. In his academic work, Drew’s biggest challenge is procrastination; getting started on tasks can be difficult, although once he begins, he’s committed to working through them until completion. His core productivity tools currently include the iOS Clock and Reminders apps for setting alarms and reminders, alongside platforms like Outlook, Gmail, Google Docs, Word, GitHub, GroupMe, and occasionally Slack for project management and team communication.

Drew is open to experimenting with new tools and custom features that might help streamline his work. He sees value in task list tools for remembering assignments and projects, especially given the number of daily group announcements he receives. While he’s not sure which new features he would ideally want, Drew appreciates the potential of Vector Space’s customizable dashboards and toolsets, which would help him and his team quickly access essential resources. Custom modules would be useful to him depending on the specific project or team, and he would be interested in settings that allow each module to be tailored further to individual needs. Drew’s ideal workspace is one that could grow with his evolving projects, enhancing focus and flexibility while providing reliable reminders and streamlined access to necessary tools.
