Persona Two

Lawrence, a computer science student and software developing engineer


Lawrence, age 23, is a software developing engineer who is completing his Master's in computer science at the University of California. He works at Facebook where he develops a website and works about 30 hours a week. He was born and raised in California and still lives there. He is completing the last semester of his Master's in Computer Science. He works both from home and at the office but prefers working from home as he hates commuting and likes working with his cats around. Despite his packed routine, Lawrence makes time for spending time with friends and sometimes dabbles in independent coding projects. Heâ€™s driven but often finds himself juggling responsibilities and deadlines across school and work with his busy schedule.

Lawrence is very involved in technology as it is the core of his career. He is quite familiar with the general Microsoft app line as well as more computer science-leaning applications. He relies on Google Calendar and Outlook to schedule his days/weeks and stay organized. Teams is his go-to for quick messaging and meetings at work, and he stores assignments and group projects in Google Drive for effective collaboration. With a focus on efficiency, Lawrence wishes he could simplify his planning processes with an optimized scheduling tool that could recommend time slots for studying and thesis work. A personalized dashboard with real-time project stats and a repository management tool would help him oversee the progress of his collaborative and work projects. These features would help him stay on top of assignments and work tasks, even during busy weeks.
