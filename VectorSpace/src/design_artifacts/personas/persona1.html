Persona One

Agnes, a compliance testing analyst


Agnes, age 25, is a compliance testing analyst at a well-known bank in Chicago. She was born and raised in a small town in Iowa but has been living in Chicago for a few years and earned her Bachelor's in Finance from the University of Chicago. She currently lives alone with her cat in a small apartment. She likes going out for dinner or coffee with her friends but appreciates spending time alone at home watching TV with her cat.

As a 25-year-old, Agnes is quite familiar with general technology. She used to use the Microsoft app line when she was a student and still uses it at work, especially Teams and Excel. She relies on Teams for communication, especially for quick check-ins, and finds Excel essential for organizing and analyzing data. Agnes would use Vector Space as she wishes her tools included features to track deadlines better, prevent important messages from getting lost, and automate regulatory updates. She would also find customizable dashboards and task lists particularly useful for monitoring compliance deadlines and identifying high-priority tasks.
