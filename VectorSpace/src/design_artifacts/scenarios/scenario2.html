Lawrence's Scenarios

Lawrence's scenario: Optimizing school and work schedule


With his finals and thesis defense approaching, Lawrence wants to ensure that he has time to study even with his busy work schedule. He wants to make sure he has time to work on homework and final projects, prepare for his thesis presentation, and also have time to study for final tests. Lawrence opens VectorSpace's optimized scheduling module where he enters his list of tasks with their priority, estimated completion times, and deadlines. The system quickly gives him the most efficient schedule where it inserts in times to study for each task such that he has sufficient time to complete them and it is before the deadline. Lawrence thinks this schedule is great and is happy that he saved time with the system and didn't feel overwhelmed by all the tasks he needed to schedule. He follows this schedule for the next couple of weeks which allows him to complete his tasks on time and stress-free because he didn't need to worry about missing a deadline or working on something very last minute.

Lawrence's scenario: Staying productive


Lawrence often struggles with staying productive while working both from home and at the office because his coworkers send him lots of messages or approach him a lot with questions or updates. To avoid these distractions and not be interrupted often, Lawrence turns on the focus mode on VectorSpace. This allows messages to still come through but not notify Lawrence every time, and also allows other coworkers to see his status and not approach him. However, if a person marks a message as urgent, Lawrence gets the message and can address it. Additionally, Lawrence uses task blocks in focus mode where he takes breaks between tasks to avoid burnout. Because he is not being constantly distracted, Lawrence is able to get through some programming uninterrupted, allowing him to stay productive and deliver quality and swift work.

Lawrence's scenario: Organizing thesis work


Lawrence is working on his Master's thesis that involves using a lot of sources. He really struggles with keeping track of his sources as well as why he is referencing each. He opens VectorSpace and creates a custom module where he can attach the PDF's of all his sources and can optionally add a summary to each as well or other notes. This allows Lawrence to quickly reference and search his notes for key arguments in certain texts especially since all sources are in one place. He also tags each source with keywords that he can use to find a source when he doesn't remember which source he needs for a certain topic. By organizing his sources, Lawrence is saving time and feeling more organized and overall more productive with his thesis process.
