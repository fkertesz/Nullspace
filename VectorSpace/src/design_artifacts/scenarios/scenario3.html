Nathaniel’s Scenarios

Nathaniel's scenario: Tracking productivity patterns


Nathaniel can often find it difficult to get started on tasks and tends to procrastinate, especially when it comes to complex assignments or long study sessions. To better understand how he can make his study sessions more efficient and get him more excited to be productive, Nathaniel turns to VectrorSpace's productivity tracking module. In this module, he can see his productive sessions logged with certain variables such as time of day, length, location, whether he is alone, and more. He can also see the trends of his study sessions visualized with colors depending on his focus. After a few weeks, he notices a pattern where he is at his most productive in the afternoons and evenings, when he is listening to music, and when he is studying with friends. With this new insight, Nathaniel changes his schedule where he studies more in the afternoons and evenings, and he invites a few friends to tag along with him as well. By aligning his study habits with the trends he could see on VectorSpace, Nathaniel can study much more efficiently, so he feels more confident and gets his procrastination under control.

Nathaniel's scenario: Team goals and metrics


One of Nathaniel’s computer engineering courses includes a big group project with biweekly smaller projects, making it crucial for his team to stay organized and on track. On VectorSpace, his team uses the team goals and metrics module, which allows each team member to set individual goals linked to the group’s broader project goals. The module provides a visual breakdown of progress for each team member, showing which tasks are completed and what still needs attention. Nathaniel finds this feature helpful as it keeps everyone accountable and provides regular updates and reminders that are visible to the whole group. This module also minimizes miscommunication by clearly displaying responsibilities and deadlines, so there is no confusion about responsibilities. By using this team module, the group works more efficiently, and Nathaniel feels confident that the project will stay on schedule without any complications or miscommunication. This tool enhances and simplifies their collaboration efforts, making teamwork smoother and more focused.

Nathaniel's scenario: Prioritized announcements and notification filtering


With his busy schedule involving several classes, clubs, and group projects, Nathaniel often finds his Outlook email and Moodle announcement page flooded with announcements, updates, and messages, many of which aren’t urgent or relevant and some are spam. VectorSpace’s prioritized announcement and notification filtering feature allows him to merge his emails and announcements and organize these notifications by importance and category, so he only receives alerts for essential updates and can see based on category whether they are relevant to him. He sets the tool to prioritize project changes, class updates, assignment deadlines, and key team announcements, while other less critical notifications are filtered for him to view later. This setup reduces distractions and helps him focus on tasks without non-urgent messages constant bothering or disturbing him. It also keeps him up-to-date on crucial updates without being overwhelmed, especially during busy weeks when he’s juggling multiple deadlines and exams. With the reduced announcements and messages, Nathaniel feels more organized and less overwhelmed.
